Blueberry Smoothie Recipe




Ingredients you'll need!


• Blueberries
• Banana
• Spinach
• Avocado
• Almond milk




Step 1

Grab your high powered blender and toss in the following ingredients: 2 cups of spinach, 2 cups of frozen blueberries, 1/2 an avocado, 1 banana, and 3-4 cups of almond milk or water

Step 2

Blend! Blend! Blend! Add more almond milk if you want a thinner consistency and more blueberries for a thicker consistency or for an even sweeter smoothie!

Step 3

Serve and enjoy! If you want to add something a little extra, add some granola, coconut shreds, or cacao nibs. Voila! A sweet yet healthy and refreshing drink that will leave you feeling energized and ready to take on the day!
